Dance Marathon

During the spring semester, Rutgers hosts the annual Rutgers Dance Marathon to raise money for the Embrace Kids Foundation, a nonprofit that seeks to address the spiritual, emotional and financial needs of patient families. Student organizations all across Rutgers raise money in the months leading up to the event. Theta Tau has brothers serving as dancers, captains, and assitant directors and is one of the most active student organizations on campus.

Habitat for Humanity Alternative Spring Break

For some, spring break is a time to kick back and relax. But for the brothers of Theta Tau its a time to get to work volunteering with other chapters alongside Habitat for Humanity. During this alternative spring break trip, brothers volunteer their time helping construct affordable housing all over the world in an effort to combat poverty housing.
